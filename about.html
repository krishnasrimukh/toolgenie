About ToolGenie

ToolGenie was created with a simple goal: to provide fast, reliable, and free online tools for everyone. Whether you need to compress images, convert file types, merge PDFs, or generate QR codes — we’ve got you covered.

We're a small, passionate team of developers who believe productivity tools should be accessible and hassle-free. Our tools are constantly evolving to stay modern, lightweight, and user-focused.

ToolGenie is completely free to use and supported by optional ads and donations. We never compromise on user privacy or add unnecessary bloat.
