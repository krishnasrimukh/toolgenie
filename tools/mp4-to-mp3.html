Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between MP4 and MP3?

MP4 is a video file format that contains both video and audio data, while MP3 is an audio-only format that contains just the audio track.

Will converting MP4 to MP3 reduce quality?

The audio quality depends on the bitrate you choose. Higher bitrates (like 320 kbps) preserve more quality but result in larger files.

Can I convert only part of a video?

Yes, you can specify start and end times to extract just a portion of the video's audio.

How long does conversion take?

Conversion time depends on your device's processing power and the length of the video. Short videos typically convert in seconds.

Are my videos stored on your servers?

No, all video processing happens directly in your browser. Your videos never leave your device.
