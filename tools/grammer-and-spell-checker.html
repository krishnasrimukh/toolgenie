Frequently Asked Questions

What languages does this tool support?

Currently, the tool primarily supports English, with basic spell checking for several other major languages. Grammar checking is most accurate for English.

Is there a limit to how much text I can check?

For performance reasons, very long texts (over 10,000 characters) may take longer to process. For best results, check one paragraph or section at a time.

How accurate is the grammar checker?

The tool provides good accuracy for common errors, but may not catch all nuanced grammar issues. Always review suggestions carefully before applying them.

Can I use this tool for professional documents?

Yes, the tool can help identify potential issues in professional writing, but should not replace human proofreading for important documents.

Is my text stored or analyzed by your servers?

No, all text processing happens directly in your browser. Your writing never leaves your computer, ensuring complete privacy.
