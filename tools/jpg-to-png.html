Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between JPG and PNG?

JPG is a lossy compression format ideal for photographs, while PNG is a lossless format that supports transparency and is better for graphics, logos, and images with text.

Will converting JPG to PNG improve quality?

No, converting from JPG to PNG won't recover lost quality from JPG compression. However, PNG will prevent further quality loss if you edit the image again.

Can I convert multiple JPGs at once?

Yes, you can select multiple JPG files when uploading, and they will be converted one by one.

Why is my PNG file larger than the original JPG?

PNG uses lossless compression, which typically results in larger file sizes than JPG's lossy compression, especially for photographic content.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
