Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats can I convert to PDF?

Our tool supports JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP image formats. You can mix different formats in a single PDF conversion.

Is there a limit to the number of images I can convert?

There's no strict limit, but very large numbers of images or very high-resolution images may cause performance issues in some browsers. For best results, we recommend converting up to 50 images at a time.

Can I rearrange the order of images in the PDF?

Yes, you can drag and drop the thumbnails in the preview area to rearrange the order before generating the PDF.

Will the quality of my images be reduced in the PDF?

The PDF will maintain the original quality of your images. No compression is applied unless you specifically choose to reduce the quality in the settings.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device unless you choose to download the PDF.
