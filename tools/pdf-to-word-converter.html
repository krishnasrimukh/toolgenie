Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the PDF to Word conversion?

The accuracy depends on the complexity of your PDF. Simple text-based PDFs convert very well, while complex layouts with multiple columns or graphics may require additional formatting in Word.

Can I convert scanned PDFs to Word?

Scanned PDFs (image-based) require OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to convert to editable text. This tool works best with text-based PDFs. For scanned documents, try our PDF to Images tool first.

Will tables and images be preserved in the Word document?

Simple tables and images may be preserved, but complex layouts might not convert perfectly. You may need to adjust the formatting in Word after conversion.

Is there a limit to the PDF size I can convert?

There's no strict limit, but very large PDFs may cause performance issues in some browsers. For best results, we recommend converting PDFs under 50 pages at a time.

Are my PDFs stored on your servers?

No, all PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
