Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the time zone conversion?

Our converter uses the most up-to-date time zone database and automatically accounts for daylight saving time changes. It's accurate for past, present, and future dates (within reasonable limits).

Can I convert times in the past or future?

Yes, you can convert times for any date. The converter accounts for historical time zone changes and daylight saving time rules that were in effect at that time.

Why do some locations show the same time but different time zones?

Some time zones share the same UTC offset but may have different names (especially during daylight saving time). Also, some locations unofficially use a neighboring time zone for convenience.

How do I find a city's time zone?

Start typing the city name in the time zone selector and matching options will appear. For smaller cities, try searching for the nearest major city in the same time zone.

Do I need to account for daylight saving time manually?

No, our converter automatically detects and applies daylight saving time rules for each location based on the date you select.
