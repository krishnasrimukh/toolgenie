How To Split PDF Pages

Step 1: Upload PDF

Click "Select PDF File" or drag and drop your PDF into the upload area.

Step 2: Choose Pages

Select the splitting method (single page, range, or multiple pages) and specify which pages to extract.

Step 3: Split

Click the "Split PDF" button to process your document.

Step 4: Download

Download individual pages or all pages as a ZIP archive.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I extract multiple non-consecutive pages?

Use the "Multiple Pages" option and enter page numbers separated by commas (e.g., 1,3,5) or ranges with hyphens (e.g., 1-3,5-8).

Is there a limit to the number of pages I can split?

There's no strict limit, but very large PDFs may cause performance issues in some browsers. For best results, we recommend splitting PDFs under 100 pages at a time.

Will splitting my PDF reduce its quality?

No, the splitting process is lossless. Your extracted pages will maintain their original quality.

Can I split password-protected PDFs?

No, this tool cannot process password-protected or encrypted PDF files.

Are my PDFs stored on your servers?

No, all PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
