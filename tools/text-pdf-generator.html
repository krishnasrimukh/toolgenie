Frequently Asked Questions

What types of text can I convert to PDF?

You can convert any plain text or formatted text to PDF. This includes articles, essays, reports, letters, resumes, and more. The editor supports basic formatting like bold, italics, underlining, and text alignment.

Can I add images to my PDF?

Yes, you can insert images into your document using the "Insert Image" button. The images will be embedded in your PDF at their original size (you may need to resize them before inserting).

Is there a limit to how much text I can convert?

There's no strict limit, but very large documents (over 50 pages) may take longer to process. For best performance, we recommend converting documents under 100 pages at a time.

Can I edit the PDF after generating it?

This tool creates read-only PDFs. If you need to make changes, you'll need to edit the original text and generate a new PDF. For advanced editing, consider using our PDF to Word Converter.

Are my documents stored on your servers?

No, all PDF generation happens directly in your browser. Your text never leaves your computer, ensuring complete privacy.
