How To Merge PDF Files

Step 1: Upload PDFs

Click "Select PDF Files" or drag and drop your PDF files into the upload area.

Step 2: Arrange Order

Drag and drop the PDF thumbnails to arrange them in your preferred order.

Step 3: Merge

Click the "Merge PDFs" button to combine your files into a single document.

Step 4: Download

Preview the merged PDF and click "Download" to save it to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many PDFs can I merge at once?

There's no strict limit, but very large numbers of PDFs or very large files may cause performance issues in some browsers. For best results, we recommend merging up to 20 PDFs at a time.

Will merging PDFs reduce their quality?

No, the merging process is lossless. Your PDFs will maintain their original quality.

Can I rearrange pages after merging?

This tool merges whole PDFs in the order you specify. For more granular control over individual pages, try our PDF Splitter tool.

Does this work with password-protected PDFs?

No, this tool cannot process password-protected or encrypted PDF files.

Are my PDFs stored on your servers?

No, all PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
