Frequently Asked Questions

How long do shortened URLs last?

Shortened URLs created with our tool remain active indefinitely. There is currently no expiration for links.

Can I edit a shortened URL after creating it?

No, once a short URL is created, it cannot be edited. You would need to create a new short URL with the updated destination.

Is there a limit to how many URLs I can shorten?

No, you can shorten as many URLs as you need. There are no usage limits.

How are click analytics tracked?

Our system records each time someone clicks your short URL. We show you the total count but don't store personal information about who clicked.

Can I use custom domains for shortened URLs?

Currently, we only support shortening to our toolgenie.in domain. Custom domain support may be added in the future.
