How To Use the Speech to Text Tool

Step 1: Allow Microphone Access

Click "Start Recording" and allow the browser to access your microphone when prompted.

Step 2: Start Speaking

Speak clearly into your microphone. Your words will appear as text in real-time.

Step 3: Stop Recording

Click "Stop Recording" when you're finished. You can then edit or copy the text.

Step 4: Use Your Text

Copy the transcribed text to your clipboard or download it as a text file.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use a good quality microphone in a quiet environment
  • Speak clearly at a natural pace
  • Position the microphone 6-12 inches from your mouth
  • Avoid background noise when possible

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the microphone working?

Make sure you've allowed microphone access when prompted. Check your browser settings if you accidentally blocked access. Also ensure no other applications are using the microphone.

How accurate is the speech recognition?

Accuracy depends on microphone quality, background noise, and how clearly you speak. In good conditions, accuracy can be over 90% for supported languages.

Can I upload an audio file to transcribe?

This version only works with live microphone input. For audio file transcription, you would need a server-based solution.

Is there a limit to recording length?

There's no strict limit, but very long sessions may cause performance issues in some browsers. For best results, keep sessions under 30 minutes.

Is my speech data being sent to servers?

No, all speech processing happens directly in your browser. Your audio never leaves your computer, ensuring complete privacy.
